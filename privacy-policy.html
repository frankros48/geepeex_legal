Privacy Policy for Inter

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Inter. This Privacy Policy explains how Sierra Capital LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Inter mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your first name, email address, date of birth (to verify you are 18 or older), up to three profile photos, and information about your interests and lifestyle preferences.
  • Location Data: Inter is a proximity-based application. We collect your real-time location coordinates (latitude and longitude) to facilitate matching you with other users within a 500-foot radius. We do not track or store your location history. Your coordinates are continuously overwritten as your location updates; we retain only your most recent active location to enable the core functionality of the App.
  • Usage and Device Information: We collect minimal technical data necessary for the App to function properly, including application error logs tied to your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the App's core proximity matching feature.
  • To create and manage your user profile.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and maintain a safe environment, including age verification and content moderation.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. We use Google Firebase solely as our backend infrastructure provider (for authentication, database hosting, and file storage). Firebase processes data in accordance with its own privacy and security standards. No other third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services are used.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may deactivate or permanently delete your account directly within the App via Settings → Account Settings → Delete Account. Upon deletion, we will permanently and immediately erase your profile, email address, photos, interests, match history, blocks, reported content, and active location data from all of our databases — with no retention period. This action is irreversible.

For additional data deletion assistance, contact us at frankros48@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

If you delete your account, all personal data associated with your account is erased immediately and permanently. We do not retain personal data after account deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

Inter is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete all associated data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
