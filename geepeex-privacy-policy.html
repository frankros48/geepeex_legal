Privacy Policy for GeePeeX

Last Updated: May 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to GeePeeX. This Privacy Policy explains how Francisco Ros ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the GeePeeX mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and optionally your first name.
  • Location Data: GeePeeX uses your device's location to place your starting point on the map and generate a running route in your area. Location is accessed only while the App is in use (foreground only). We do not track or store your location history beyond the routes you explicitly export.
  • Route Data: When you export a route to GPX format, we store the coordinate path of that route in your account history so you can replay it in the App. This data is associated with your account and is not shared with third parties.
  • Payment Information: GPX export is a one-time paid feature ($4.99). Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card number or payment details — only a record of whether your account has unlocked the feature.
  • Usage Data: We collect minimal technical data necessary for the App to function, including error logs tied to your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized running routes based on your location and preferences.
  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To process your one-time GPX export purchase and unlock the feature on your account.
  • To store your past exported routes so you can access them in the App.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and provide customer support.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. We use the following third-party services solely to operate the App:

  • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider for authentication and database hosting. Data is processed in accordance with Supabase's privacy and security standards.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for the one-time GPX export purchase. Stripe processes your payment information under its own privacy policy and PCI compliance standards. We receive only a confirmation of payment status.
  • Mapbox: Used to calculate running route geometry. Your approximate start location is sent to Mapbox to generate route options. No account data is shared.
  • Google Maps: Used to display the map within the App. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at frankros48@gmail.com. Upon deletion, we will permanently erase your profile, email address, route history, and payment status from our databases.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, all personal data is erased permanently. We do not retain personal data after account deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

GeePeeX is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a user is under 13, we will immediately terminate their account and delete all associated data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: frankros48@gmail.com
