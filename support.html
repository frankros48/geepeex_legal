Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a route?

Tap the map to place your start point, choose your distance, then tap Generate Route. You can also draw a boundary to keep your route in a specific area.

How do I export a GPX file?

After generating a route, tap Export GPX. A one-time $4.99 purchase unlocks GPX export for your account permanently.

I paid but GPX export is still locked.

The unlock is processed via Stripe webhook and usually applies within a few seconds. Close and reopen the app and try again. If it's still locked after a few minutes, email us with your receipt and we'll sort it out.

How do I move my start point?

Long-press anywhere on the map to move the pin, or drag the pin directly.

How do I request a refund?

Contact Apple Support for App Store purchases, or email us directly and we'll help.
